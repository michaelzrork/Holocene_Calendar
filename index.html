The Holocene Calendar

12,000 Years of Human Era

Scroll through civilization — to scale

The Holocene Calendar adds 10,000 years to the common era, placing all of human civilization on one continuous timeline.

Year 1 CE becomes 10,001 HE. Today is year 12,026 HE.

This timeline is proportionally accurate.
Scroll distance = time elapsed.
100 years = 400 pixels
